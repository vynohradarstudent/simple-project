What is lethal Company? And his a secret history

Lethal Company is a 2023 multiplayer horror video game currently in Early Access on Steam developed by an indie developer named Zeekers. The project combines the following genres: survival horror, roguelike and strategy.

Developer: Zeekerss

Publisher: Zeekerss, SniPro Games

Release date: October 9, 2023.

Mode: Multiplayer, Single player

Nominations: BAFTA Games EE Players' Choice Award

Genres: Indie games, Adventure video game, Survival horror, Adventure, Early Access

Platforms: Microsoft Windows
